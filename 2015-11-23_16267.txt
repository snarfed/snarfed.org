Which one are you?

1. *Never-better.* We've never had it so good.
1. *Better-never.* If only all this progress had never happened!
1. *Ever-waser.* All of this is as it ever was.

Great categorization of views by [Adam Gopnik](http://www.adamgopnik.com/) in his New Yorker piece *[The Information](http://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2011/02/14/the-information)*.
