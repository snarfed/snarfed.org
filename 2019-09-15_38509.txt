"Climate change" tricks us into complacency by framing it as slow, diffuse, and maybe not even bad.

<a href="https://grist.org/article/is-it-time-to-retire-climate-change-for-climate-crisis/">Call it "climate crisis" instead.</a> Reinforce that it's already hurting everyone and must be addressed now. Use our brain's flaws for good. Language matters!
