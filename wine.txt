wine

<p class="right"><img src="/wine.jpg" alt="wine" /></p>

Notes on wine I've tried.
<good> I liked,
<ok> were just ok,
<bad> I didn't like.
Also see [beer](/beer), [chocolate](/chocolate), and
[other lists](/lists).

Naturally, this isn't comprehensive - I've tried wines that aren't listed here -
and it's biased toward my taste. If I mark a wine <nobr><bad>,</nobr>
I may still think it's good overall, but just doesn't match my own personal
taste, and vice versa. I tend to drink big, sharp, spicy reds.
