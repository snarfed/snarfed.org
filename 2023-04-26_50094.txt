<div class="wp-block-jetpack-markdown"><p>Richard Dawkins <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meme#Dawkins">coined the term <em>meme</em></a> in his 1976 book <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Selfish_Gene"><em>The Selfish Gene</em></a> to mean &quot;a unit of cultural transmission, or a unit of imitation.&quot;</p>
<p>I wondered what he'd think of it evolving to mean primarily &quot;silly bad internet joke&quot; these days. <a href="https://twitter.com/RichardDawkins/status/276449081746915328">Evidently he told us what he thought, somewhat, back in 2012</a>:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>It is very good that Wikipedia also gives a page to &quot;Internet Meme&quot;. Internet memes are arguably the most important subset of memes today.</p>
</blockquote>
