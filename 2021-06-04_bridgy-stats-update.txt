It's been a year since I last looked at [Bridgy](https://brid.gy/) usage stats, so here's an update!

The most noticeable event [since last year](/2020-06-27_bridgy-stats-update-5) is that [Instagram finally blocked our server side scraping for good](https://github.com/snarfed/bridgy/issues/665). We quickly [launched a browser extension for both Instagram and Facebook](/2021-01-05_bridgy-browser-extension-for-instagram), which is working well, but meant we had start to all over again with adoption and growth. Ah well, silos gonna silo.

We also hit another [webmention milestone](https://snarfed.org/1-million-webmentions): over 2M webmentions successfully sent. Congrats!

One interesting mystery is the sudden drop in publish growth in September 2020. Before then, we were seeing steady exponential growth in the number of posts published, peaking at almost 8k per month in August 2020. That suddenly dropped to less than 2k per month by October, and has stayed there since. No clue why. Anyone have any ideas?
