[Beer I'd like to try](#to_try) and
[beer I've tried](#tried).
<good> I liked,
<ok> were just ok,
<bad> I didn't like.
Also see [wine](/wine), [chocolate](/chocolate), and
[other lists](/lists).

Naturally, this isn't comprehensive - I've tried others that aren't listed
here - and it's biased toward my taste. If I mark a beer <nobr><bad>,</nobr>
I may still think it's good overall, but just doesn't match my own personal
taste, and vice versa. I tend to prefer IPAs and darker beers like stouts and
porters. I don't mind lighter ales and lagers, but some, like lambics, I'm not a
fan of.
