It's that time of year again...[Bridgy](https://brid.gy/) stats update! Top line numbers: 6471 accounts, 28M silo responses, 4.7M links, 2.3M webmentions sent, 99k posts published, 102k [blog posts processed](https://brid.gy/about#blogs), 16k [blog webmentions](https://brid.gy/about#blogs) received.
