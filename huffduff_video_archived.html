huffduff-video

Extracts the audio from videos on YouTube, Vimeo, and many more sites and sends it to Huffduffer.

huffduff-video has been decommissioned. So long, and thanks for all the fish!

For alternatives, check out Breadcrum, PodQueue, Podify, and youtube-dl-api-server.


Archived source code at github.com/snarfed/huffduff-video.
